About Us

Where is this store based? Dwaekki is based in South Korea. If you want to know more about us, visit our contact page!

Shipping

What are your shipping costs & times?

Usually, we process your orders within 3-10 business days and the deliveries take 2-5 days (can take up to 10 business days or more) for express shipping. However, shipping times also depend on what you purchased or where you live!


Will I be able to track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped we'll update you with a tracking number. A link to the tracking page will be supplied through the shipment email.


My order isn't here yet, what's up?

Shipping times vary based on a number of things including logistics, weather, customs, etc. Contact us if your order hasn't arrived within 15 days so we can figure out what's happening. If it's been less than 10 days, your package may be still in transit, so please give it a little more time for it to arrive at your door.

Returns/exchanges

What is your return policy?

All products sold on Dwaekki include a 10-day money back guarantee. You can read more about our return policy here.

Do you allow exchanges?

Sure, they follow the same rules as our returns.

Payment

What are my payment options?

We accept paypal, credit cards and debit cards.

Is my information safe?

Of course it is! All your payment information is encrypted and processed through paypal, the most trusted online payment solutions on the Web. The only information we store is your name, email, and address for obvious reasons.

Other

Why did i get a different size product from what I ordered?

I'm afraid the short answer for this question is that you didn't. It is very important that you carefully check the measurement on the product page before you determine which size to order. If you are from a non-asian country, the measurements may differ (ounces,ml).

I want to cancel my order. What should I do?

Cancellation request must be requested immediately after receiving the order confirmation email. (please note that if the order is already processed, we may not be able to cancel your order.)

Why did I receive a refund/partial refund?

Sometimes we automatically issue refunds to you due to shipping delays, depleted stocks or other issues with shipping and delivery. We will refund you via the original manner in which you paid for the relevant order. Unfortunately, we cannot direct refunds to different accounts or payment methods.

What if i need to make a change to my order?

You received your confirmation email and noticed that there is something wrong with your shipping address or the item, color or size is wrong? Contact us(hellodwaekki@gmail.com) immediately so we can help you fix it. (once again, if the order is already processed, we may not be able to make changes as requested.)
