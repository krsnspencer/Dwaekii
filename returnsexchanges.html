Returns/Exchanges/Refunds

Return requirements:

Return requests must be made within the first 3 days after delivery.

Returns must be approved by Dwaekki before shipment.

Returned items must be in original condition, and contain both the original parts and the packaging.

Returns should be sent postpaid if the reason for returning is not from damaged (defective) merchandise. (exchanges will be made after the original product is returned and examined, and after the re-shipping fee is paid.)

The returning shipping fee will only be reimbursed when the return or exchange is a result of our error or damaged (defective) merchandise and when only the fee does not exceed 50% of the total amount of the product.

A shipping fee will be deducted from your refund and will only be refunded when the return is a result of our error or damaged merchandise.
*for damaged, defective, or missing merchandise
contact us immediately after you received the parcel. (the claim can only be accepted within 10 days after the delivery.)
make sure you do not throw out the package (outer box), bubble wraps, and the packing slip which will be required for the investigation.
Any quantity problem of product or package (ex. Missing item), contact us with the unboxing video proof; pictures or any other proofing formats will not be accepted.

How to return:

To make a return request, please contact us within 10 days of delivery. In your message, please include your order number, the products you wish to return, and why. Once we approve your request, we will supply you with our returns address. Note: Dwaekki is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged shipments for returning packages. If this worries you, a tracking number and insurance would be a good investment.

After return:

Once your return is received, we will examine and process your refund. From here, we will either accept or reject your return and email you with our decision. If we have determined that your return meets the requirements above, you will be refunded
- fully, if the return was a result of our error.
- partially (shipping costs deducted), if the return was not a result of our error.
